🧠 Mini AI Hook Correction Reference (IS 2502:2014)
For 180° bend (standard hook): extra length = 11.87d per hook. For 90° bend: extra = 11d per bend. For stirrups with 135° hooks: add 24d for both ends combined. Mini AI applies these automatically when generating BBS from drawings.
