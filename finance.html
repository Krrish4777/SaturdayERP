SATURDAY Rules — Rate Application Logic
RULE GST-001 · WORKS CONTRACT RATE
12% GST on Works Contracts to Government / Semi-Government Bodies
When SaturdayERP+ detects that the client is a Government entity (TGEWIDC, TSIIC, HMDA, NHAI, CPWD, any State PWD, Municipal bodies), SATURDAY automatically applies 12% GST on all Works Contract invoices (RA Bills). Applicable under Notification No. 11/2017-Central Tax (Rate) — Serial No. 3(xii).
🤖 SATURDAY Auto-applies Notification 11/2017-CT(Rate) Rate: 12% (6% CGST + 6% SGST)
RULE GST-002 · COMPOSITE SUPPLY
12% GST on Composite Works Contract (Material + Labour)
Construction contracts that are composite supply (both material and labour involved) to Government entities are taxed at 12%. SATURDAY detects this from the BOQ structure — when materials are supplied along with labour, the entire contract value is taxed at 12%, not split. Pure material supply is taxed at respective material rates (28% cement, 18% steel).
🤖 SATURDAY Auto-applies GST Act Section 8 Trigger: BOQ has both material + service
RULE GST-003 · SUBCONTRACTOR RATE
18% GST on Subcontractor Services (B2B)
When you receive services from subcontractors (masonry, bar bending, plastering, shuttering), GST is 18% if the main contractor is registered. SATURDAY applies 18% on all subcontractor bills marked as B2B service. The ITC of 18% paid to subcontractor can be offset against 12% collected from client (subject to ITC rules).
🤖 SATURDAY Auto-applies Rate: 18% ITC Available
RULE GST-004 · MATERIAL PURCHASE RATES
Material-specific GST Rates for ITC Tracking
SATURDAY applies and tracks material-specific GST rates for ITC: Cement (OPC, PPC, Slag) — 28% | TMT Steel bars — 18% | River Sand, M-Sand — Nil (exempt in most states) | Aggregates (20mm, 12mm) — 5% | Fly Ash Blocks — 12% | Binding Wire — 18% | Plywood/Shuttering — 18% | Paint, Putty — 18%/28%.
🤖 SATURDAY Tracks per Material Cement: 28% | Steel: 18% | Sand: Nil
RULE GST-WC-001
Definition of Works Contract under GST (Section 2(119))
Works contract means a contract for building, construction, fabrication, completion, erection, installation, fitting out, improvement, modification, repair, maintenance, renovation, alteration or commissioning of any immovable property wherein transfer of property in goods (whether as goods or in some other form) is involved in the execution of such contract.

Key implication: All construction contracts are Works Contracts. SATURDAY treats them as composite supply of services (principal) + goods.
🤖 SATURDAY Classification Auto-triggerCGST Act 2017 Sec 2(119)
RULE GST-WC-002
Time of Supply for Works Contract Services
Time of supply for Works Contract is earliest of: (a) Date of invoice (RA Bill submission), or (b) Date of completion of service, or (c) Date of receipt of payment. SATURDAY raises GST liability at the time of RA Bill preparation, not when payment is received. This ensures GSTR-1 is always accurate regardless of payment delays from TGEWIDC.
🤖 SATURDAY Uses RA Bill DateCGST Act Sec 13(2)
RULE GST-WC-003
Retention Money — GST Treatment
Retention money deducted by client (TGEWIDC typically deducts 5%) is NOT separate supply. GST is charged on the full RA Bill amount before retention deduction. When retention is released (typically at project completion), no fresh GST liability arises. SATURDAY tracks retention separately and does NOT generate a fresh GST entry when retention is released.
🤖 SATURDAY Excludes Retention from GSTCircular No. 76/50/2018-GST
RULE GST-WC-004
Mobilisation Advance — GST on Advance
When mobilisation advance is received from client before any work, GST is payable at the time of receipt of advance (not at billing). SATURDAY generates a Receipt Voucher with GST liability on any advance received. Invoice for the advance must be raised within 30 days or the advance receipt date becomes the tax point.
🤖 SATURDAY Generates GST on Advance ReceiptCGST Rule 47
RULE GST-WC-005
Place of Supply — Works Contract on Immovable Property
For works contract services related to immovable property, place of supply is where the immovable property is located (CGST Act Sec 12(3)). For YIIRS Pkg 05 in Nizamabad, Telangana — the place of supply is Telangana. This means SGST goes to Telangana Government. SATURDAY uses project location to determine SGST/CGST split (or IGST for inter-state).
🤖 SATURDAY Uses Project LocationCGST Act Sec 12(3)
RULE ITC-001 — CORE ITC RULE
ITC Available: 18% GST Paid on Subcontractor vs 12% Collected from Client
Construction companies face an inverted duty structure: You collect 12% GST on RA Bills from client but pay 18% GST to subcontractors. The excess ITC (18% paid - 12% collected) can be: (a) offset against other output tax liabilities, (b) claimed as refund under Section 54(3) of CGST Act. SATURDAY tracks this inverted duty position and alerts when refund claim becomes viable.
🤖 SATURDAY Tracks Inverted Duty PositionRefund u/s 54(3)Key for cash flow
RULE ITC-002 — BLOCKED ITC
ITC NOT Available on These Inputs (Section 17(5))
The following inputs do NOT qualify for ITC even if GST is paid: (a) Works contract services for construction of immovable property (own use), (b) Motor vehicles (except those used in specified manner), (c) Food and beverages, club memberships, (d) Construction material used for own building/office, (e) Travel benefits to employees. SATURDAY automatically blocks ITC on these categories and does NOT include them in ITC register.
🚫 SATURDAY Blocks TheseCGST Act Sec 17(5)
RULE ITC-003 — ITC MATCHING
ITC Must Match GSTR-2B (Supplier's GSTR-1)
From FY 2022-23, ITC can only be claimed on invoices appearing in GSTR-2B (auto-populated from supplier's GSTR-1/IFF). If supplier has not filed GSTR-1, ITC is not available to you even if you have the invoice. SATURDAY cross-checks your purchase invoices against GSTR-2B data downloaded from GST portal and flags any mismatches before you file GSTR-3B.
🤖 SATURDAY Auto-Reconciles with GSTR-2BCGST Rule 36(4)
RULE ITC-004 — ITC PROPORTIONATE
Proportionate ITC When Inputs Used for Both Taxable and Exempt Supplies
If you execute both taxable (12% GST) and exempt (Nil GST — affordable housing) projects, ITC must be apportioned. ITC attributable to exempt supply is NOT available. SATURDAY computes the proportion based on turnover from taxable vs exempt supplies and restricts ITC accordingly using Rule 42/43 calculations.
🤖 SATURDAY Computes Proportionate ITCCGST Rule 42/43
RULE RCM-001
Reverse Charge on Goods Transport Agency (GTA)
If you hire a Goods Transport Agency (truck/lorry) to transport construction materials and the GTA is NOT registered under GST, YOU (the recipient) must pay GST under Reverse Charge Mechanism at 5% (without ITC) or 12% (with ITC). SATURDAY detects transport payments to unregistered transporters and auto-generates RCM liability.
🤖 SATURDAY Auto-generates RCM for TransportNotification 13/2017-CT(Rate)Rate: 5% or 12%
RULE RCM-002
Reverse Charge on Legal Services from Advocate
Legal services received from an individual advocate or partnership firm are subject to RCM at 18%. If you pay legal/arbitration fees to a lawyer for dispute resolution on a project, RCM applies. SATURDAY prompts RCM entry when vendor category is marked as "Legal / Advocate".
🤖 SATURDAY Flags Legal PaymentsRCM: 18%
RULE RCM-003
Reverse Charge on Purchases from Unregistered Dealers (Section 9(4))
If you purchase goods/services from an UNREGISTERED supplier, RCM applies on you (the recipient). However, as per current notification, this is currently applicable only for specified categories. SATURDAY flags unregistered vendor payments and alerts whether RCM applies based on the nature of supply.
🤖 SATURDAY Checks Vendor GST StatusCGST Act Sec 9(4)
EXEMPTION GST-E001
Pure Labour Services to Government — EXEMPT
Services provided to Government/Government Entity consisting PURELY of labour (no material) for construction/repair of infrastructure are EXEMPT from GST (Nil rated). If TGEWIDC gives you a labour-only portion of the contract (pure service), no GST is applicable. SATURDAY classifies this when the BOQ shows 0% material component and marks the bill as Nil-rated.
🤖 SATURDAY Checks BOQ Material %Notification 12/2017-IT(Rate) Sl. 3
EXEMPTION GST-E002
Affordable Residential Housing — GST Exemption / 1%
Construction of affordable residential apartments (carpet area ≤ 60 sqm in metros, ≤ 90 sqm in other cities, value ≤ ₹45L) is exempt or 1% GST without ITC. If any project in SaturdayERP+ is marked as "Affordable Housing" in Admin → Projects, SATURDAY applies the appropriate exemption/reduced rate automatically.
🤖 SATURDAY Checks Project TypeNotification 03/2019-CT(Rate)
EXEMPTION GST-E003
River Sand / Natural Sand — Exempt (in Telangana)
Natural sand, including river sand, is EXEMPT from GST in most transactions. Purchases of river sand should not carry GST. However, M-Sand (manufactured sand) attracts 5% GST. SATURDAY distinguishes between River Sand (Nil) and M-Sand (5%) in the Stock Register and applies accordingly in ITC calculations.
🤖 SATURDAY Distinguishes Sand TypesNil — Chapter 26 of GST Tariff
PENALTY GST-P001 — LATE FILING
Late Filing of GSTR-1: ₹50/day (Nil return: ₹20/day)
Penalty: ₹25/day CGST + ₹25/day SGST = ₹50/day maximum. For Nil return: ₹10 + ₹10 = ₹20/day. Maximum penalty: ₹5,000. SATURDAY sends alerts 7 days before due date, 3 days before, and on due date to prevent this. If you miss the date, SATURDAY calculates the penalty automatically.
⚠️ SATURDAY Sends 3 Advance AlertsCGST Act Sec 47(1)
PENALTY GST-P002 — LATE PAYMENT INTEREST
Interest on Late Payment of GST: 18% per annum
If GST is not paid by the due date (20th of month for GSTR-3B), interest at 18% per annum applies on the unpaid tax amount from due date to payment date. For excess ITC claims: 24% interest. SATURDAY tracks this and includes interest calculation in its cash flow forecast to show the true cost of delayed payment.
🤖 SATURDAY Calculates Interest AutoCGST Act Sec 50
PENALTY GST-P003 — INCORRECT ITC CLAIM
Wrongly Availed ITC: Penalty Equal to ITC Amount + Interest
If ITC is claimed on blocked credits (Section 17(5)), RCM not paid, or ITC availed before supplier files GSTR-1 — penalty equals the ITC amount plus 18% interest. SATURDAY's GSTR-2B reconciliation prevents this by only allowing ITC on matched invoices from supplier's filings.
🤖 SATURDAY Prevents Wrong ITCCGST Act Sec 74

🤖 How SATURDAY Applies TDS Rules
1. When you record a vendor payment, SATURDAY checks vendor's registration type (Company vs Individual) from the vendor master.
2. SATURDAY checks the section — 194C for contractors, 194I for equipment rental, 194J for CA/legal fees.
3. SATURDAY checks the threshold — if single payment > ₹30,000 or aggregate in FY > ₹1,00,000, TDS is deducted.
4. TDS is auto-calculated, deducted from net payment, and posted as "TDS Payable" in the ledger.
5. SATURDAY maintains the TDS register and auto-prepares Form 26Q at quarter end.
6. Challan for deposit is auto-generated on 7th of the following month.
